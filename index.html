About Mondirandom

Mondirandom Gallery is a collection of the most striking and enigmatic paintings by Piet Mondirandom (1872 - 1944). The colors, shapes, textures and patterns all contribute to the strange beauty of his vision, which is shaped by uncanny geometry and bewildering simplicity. Each of the infinitely many images featured in this collection was hand curated and available for download as wallpaper for your desktop or mobile.
